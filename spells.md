Let's add second set of non combat abilities. Those abilities will  
be in the future core mechanic of mage character class, but other classes also can   
use abilities in narrower manner. Abilities can be scoped to building, map or        
character, meaning mage can cast some ability that will: 

Enchant movement speed for specific player or self.

Those scond abiities are calle SPELLS, are trained like Skills but with Spell books (instead of skill books), so new type of items is needed
Training spells is the same as skills.

All spells have duration in seconds, displayed in human readible format e.g 1h 19min

Spells effects:

1. +X% attack, defence, critical chance, critical damage
2. Heal Y amount
3. Give X movement speed
4. Give Z energy

Defining spells:

Admin can add and manage spells in similiar manner to abilities. Spell can have some cost associated, it's not mana like for abilities but resources - items or gold.
E.g

Casting level 1 "haste" requires 2x iron bar
Casting level 2 "haste" requires 3x iron bar

Spells can be viewed and used on self by player in new tab located next to Loadout

it opens window with all available spells showing current spell level, spell icon and name. Clicking on spell shows separate window with more dtails, description, detailed stats, next level bonuses and "Cast" button, that when clicked casts spell (if player has enough resources).

If player has a spell active on him, it is showed in character stats panel, on the bottom, represented by spell icon, progress bar that shows remaining time. On hover it shows more details in game stylized tooltip.

If player is already under spell effect and new is cast, it is replaced only when new spell is qual of same power, it's not possible to replace haste level 2 with haste level 1

Casting spell on self:
From spells window, button next to spell "cast"

Casting spells on other players:
From player details menu, when player is in the same location and player details menu is opened, it shows all available spells and player can click on it casting it and buffing other player.
